About

Summary

Welcome to our gallery, a vibrant showcase of ten featured sea and air creatures,

each brought to life with smooth CSS Grid layouts and eye-catching animations.

Our gallery takes you from the mysterious depths of coral reefs to the boundless skies above,

highlighting the beauty and diversity of marine and avian life.

 

Mission

This gallery mainly aims to foster appreciation for ocean and avian biodiversity, hoping to

inspire viewers to conserve action.

Marine species are disappearing at alarming rates—up to 31 % of fish stocks are overexploited—

and bird populations face habitat loss across the globe.

By combining stunning imagery with cutting-edge web design, we hope to spark curiosity,

raise awareness, and encourage visitors to learn more about these remarkable creatures.

Join us in celebrating and protecting the natural world.
